# Squibbon — Shop Management architecture & mockups

Product specs (PRDs) and on-brand screen mockups for extending **Squibbon** (a Canadian Ontario/Quebec Transport Management System) into a **complete auto repair-shop platform** — modelled on Shopmonkey/Tekmetric/Shop-Ware/Mitchell/Fullbay and tuned for Canada (bilingual EN/FR, ON/QC compliance).

Mockups are static SVGs on the Squibbon client design system (Spike / Flowbite-React, purple `#c479c2` / teal `#16CDC7`, Plus Jakarta Sans). Open any `.svg` in a browser; export PNG/PDF for Notion cases. Data shown is illustrative placeholder.

## What makes Squibbon different (Canada)

Bilingual EN/FR throughout · Ontario DriveON & Quebec SAAQ inspection compliance · **repair-transaction law enforced in software** (ON 10% / QC 0% estimate cap, QC statutory warranty) · provincial tax & tire eco-fees · tire hotel for the winter-tire seasons · and fleet/heavy-duty service fused with the TMS Squibbon already runs.
